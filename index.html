About Noor Al Amal

Noor Al Amal is an automobile corporate business group focused on building a complete ecosystem for vehicle parts, service, trading, franchise operations, and global automobile supply. We aim to serve customers, businesses, garages, suppliers, fleet owners, and investors with trust, transparency, and long-term value.

Mission

To build a trusted and scalable automobile business ecosystem that provides quality spare parts, reliable vehicle service, organized workshop networks, transparent used vehicle trading, and strong global sourcing partnerships.

Vision

To become a leading automobile corporate network recognized for service quality, parts reliability, franchise growth, trading transparency, and international automobile business partnerships.
